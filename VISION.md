# one — vision

## What one becomes

An autonomous research and engineering engine that compounds knowledge
until it solves the problem. Any problem. Any domain. Any scale.

Run it on cancer research — it pulls papers from bio, tech, holistic
medicine, connects patterns across disciplines nobody has connected,
grows its knowledge graph, and keeps going until it finds something real.

Run it on AGI — it explores HDC, Tsetlin Machines, Active Inference,
neuroscience, category theory, whatever is needed, building understanding
layer by layer until the architecture emerges.

Run it on your codebase — it learns every decision, every bug, every
architectural choice, and uses that compounded knowledge to build
better software every day.

## The principle

Intelligence = accumulated knowledge + cross-domain reasoning + relentless iteration

One provides all three:
- Knowledge accumulates in the graph (memories, rules, entities, research)
- Cross-domain reasoning comes from graph traversal (patterns in biology
  connect to patterns in computing)
- Relentless iteration comes from the auto loop (no stopping, no breaks,
  no "good enough")
